Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This policy explains how the Superpicks mobile application ("the App"), published by Synergy ("we", "us"), handles data. It applies to the App only.

The short version. Superpicks has no sign-up, no login and no profiles. We do not ask you for your name, email address or any other personal detail, and we do not collect any directly. The data described below is collected automatically by our advertising and messaging providers, and is not linked to your identity.

1. Data we collect ourselves

None. The App contains no registration, no account system and no forms. You can use every feature without providing any information about yourself.

2. Data collected by third parties in the App

We use a small number of third-party services to run the App. These services collect data automatically:

Google AdMob (advertising)

We display banner advertising from Google AdMob to users who are not Pro subscribers. Ads are removed entirely for active Superpicks Pro subscribers. AdMob collects:

DataPurpose
Device identifiers (including the advertising identifier)Serving and measuring ads; analytics
IP address, used to estimate approximate (city-level) locationServing regionally relevant ads; analytics
Advertising data (ads shown, impressions, taps)Ad delivery and measurement
Product interactions (app opens, taps)Analytics
Performance data (launch time, hangs, energy use)Analytics; app functionality
Crash logsDiagnostics

We do not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt and we do not request permission to track you. Advertising in the App is therefore non-personalised. We do not use your data for tracking as Apple defines it, and we do not sell your data or share it with data brokers.

Google's handling of this data is governed by its own policy: policies.google.com/privacy. Information on how Google uses data from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Firebase Cloud Messaging (notifications)

If you allow notifications, Firebase Cloud Messaging assigns your device a push token so we can alert you when a new tip is published. The token identifies a device, not a person. We do not attach any personal information to it. Notifications are optional and you can turn them off at any time in iOS Settings.

Firebase Firestore (tip content)

Tips and results are stored in Firebase Firestore and delivered to the App. This is a one-way transfer: content is downloaded to your device. No information about you is uploaded.

Firebase Authentication (staff only)

The App includes a private publishing tool that only we can access, using our own credentials, to post and settle tips. It is not available to users and collects no user data.

Football data provider

Live scores, fixtures, tables and player statistics are retrieved from API-Football (api-sports.io) under a paid licence. Your device requests match data; no information about you is sent to this provider.

3. Linking and tracking

Because the App has no accounts, none of the data above is linked to your identity. We do not build user profiles, and we do not combine data across apps or websites for advertising.

4. Children

Superpicks is rated 18+ and is intended solely for adults. It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has used the App, contact us and we will assist.

5. Your choices

  • Advertising identifier — you can reset or limit it in iOS Settings, under Privacy & Security.
  • Notifications — turn off at any time in iOS Settings under Superpicks.
  • Remove ads — subscribe to Superpicks Pro.
  • Stop all collection — delete the App from your device.

6. Retention

We hold no personal data, so there is nothing for us to retain or delete on your behalf. Data collected by Google is retained according to Google's own policies.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal data held about you, or to object to its processing. As we hold no personal data about you, requests of this kind are best directed to Google, whose services collect the data described above. You are welcome to contact us and we will help where we can.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as the App changes. The date at the top shows when it was last revised.
