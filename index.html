Common questions

How do I cancel Superpicks Pro?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions, then Superpicks. Cancel at least 24 hours before your period ends to avoid renewing. Cancelling is handled by Apple, not by us.

I subscribed but Pro isn't showing.

Open Superpicks, go to Settings, and tap "Restore purchases". If it still doesn't appear, email us with the Apple Account you used.

Why are the Premier League tables empty?

The major European leagues run from August to May. Between seasons there are no matches played, so there is no table to show. Pick MLS, Brazil or Norway to see in-season data.

How do I turn notifications on or off?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, find Superpicks, and use the Notifications switch. Notifications are optional and only alert you when a new tip is published.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple Account.

Do you take bets?

No. Superpicks publishes predictions and football data for entertainment only. We are not a bookmaker, we accept no wagers or deposits, and we pay out no winnings.
